Upload your fit note

Choose your fit note

By clicking 'Choose File' you can upload a picture or scan of your fit note directly from your phone, tablet or computer. For phone and tablet users you also have the option to directly take a photo of your fit note.

Guidance on uploading your fit note

! Warning Make sure that the image of your fit note is 'landscape', not blurred and has no shadows.

To successfully upload your fit note:

  • the file must be a JPG, BMP, PNG, TIF or PDF
  • the file must be smaller than 10MB
  • all 4 corners of the fit note must be visible within the image
I am having difficulty in uploading my fit note

If you have difficulty in uploading your fit note, post your fit note to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do note need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy of your fit note for your own records.

Talk to an advisor
