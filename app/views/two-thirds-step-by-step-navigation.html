Save and upload your fit note

  1. Take a photo of your fit note or scan it to your computer
  2. Make sure:
    • you're in a well lit place
    • all of your fit note is in the image
  3. Save the photo or scan of your fit note somewhere you can easily find it on your computer

Your photo or scan will be automatically checked to make sure it's good enough.

  1. Step 1 Hold the device directly above the fit note

    It is best hold the device about a foot above the fit note and avoid taking the photo at an angle

  2. Step 2 Find a well lit place and avoid shadows

    2. Make sure:
      • your fit note is well lit
      • the device screen is bright enough to see the image of your fit note clearly
      • nothing is between your fit note and the light that casts a strong shadow

  3. Step 3 Make sure your fit note is sideways or 'landscape'

  4. Step 4 Make sure the whole thing is in the photo

    The fit note should fill as much of the photo as possible but all 4 corners should be inside it.

  5. Step 5 Make sure the photo isn't blurred

    If you're not sure how to get a clear, sharp image, check your device manual.
