This service can only process fit notes that are on one single page

My fit note is not on one single page

Your doctor may have provided you with a fit note that is:

  • on two separate pages
  • printed on the front and back of the same piece of paper

These fit notes cannot be processed.

Next steps

In order to progress your claim, you can contact your doctor for a fit note that is on one single page.

Alternatively you can post your fit note, but payment may be delayed to allow for delivery and processing.

Post your fit note

You can post your fit note to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy of your fit note for your own record (for example a photo).
