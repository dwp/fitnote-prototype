How to upload your fit note

  1. Open your email sent to you by your doctor or GP practice containing your fit note.
  2. Download the fit note which was attached from your doctor or GP practice's email.
  3. Upload your fit note. Your fit note may be saved in your device's Downloads, Recents or Saved As folder.
