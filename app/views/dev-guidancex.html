We can only accept a Statement of Fitness for work.

If you have tried to upload your fit note multiple times, it could be in the wrong format, have missing information or unable to be processed by our system.

If it is a digital fit note, you can print it, take a photo of it and upload the photo.

Alternatively, you can send us your fit note instead.

Where to send your medical evidence

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp.

Make sure you keep a copy of any medical evidence for your own record.
