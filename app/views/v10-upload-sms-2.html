Upload your fit note

  1. Open your text message sent to you by your doctor or GP practice containing your fit note.
  2. Click on the link within your text message from your doctor or GP practice.
  3. Download your fit note onto your device. For more information of how to download your fit note, please look at your device manual
  4. Upload your fit note. Your fit note may be saved in your device's Downloads, Recents or Saved As folder

Your file will automatically be checked to make sure the quality is good enough.

  • the selected file must be a JPG, BMP, PNG, TIF or PDF
  • the selected file must be smaller than 10MB
  • ensure all 4 corners of the fit note are within the image
