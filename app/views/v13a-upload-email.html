Upload your fit note

Follow these instructions to upload your fit note:

  1. Open your email sent to you by your doctor or GP practice containing your fit note.
  2. Download the fit note which was attached on your email. This may automatically be added to your 'Downloads', Recents' or 'Saved As' folder.
  3. Upload your fit note.

Choose a file to upload

Your photo will go through a basic technical check.

In order to upload your fit note successfully:

  • the file must be a JPG, BMP. PNG, TIF or PDF
  • the file must be smaller than 10MB
  • all 4 corners of the fit note must be within the file
I am having difficulty in uploading my fit note

If you have difficulty in uploading your fit note, post your fit note to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do note need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy of your fit note for your own records.
