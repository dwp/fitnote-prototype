This service can only process fit notes that are on one single page

Your doctor may have provided you with a fit note that is:

  • on two separate pages
  • is printed on the front and back of the same piece of paper

These fit notes cannot be processed by this service, but can be posted.

Post your fit note

To process your claim further, post your fit note to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy or photograph of your fit note for your own records.
