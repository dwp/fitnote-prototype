Only a fit note can be uploaded and processed by this service

If you do not have a fit note and do require one, please get in touch with your doctor or GP practice. The following are examples of a medical letter, isolation note and MED 10, they are not fit notes and cannot be uploaded onto this service.

However you can post alternative medical evidence, such as a medical letter, isolation note or MED 10 to Freepost DWP ESA 26. Please ensure that you have a copy or duplicate of your medical evidence before sending it.

How to post medical evidence
Medical evidence can be posted directly to ESA using the address Freepost DWP ESA 26. You do not need a postage stamp.
