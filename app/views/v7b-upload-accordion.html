Save and upload your fit note

There are several ways for you to upload your fit note depending on how you have received it.

If you need help, please click on one of the below options which will provide guidance on how to upload your fit note.

I have a paper copy of my fit note

  1. Take a photo of your fit note or scan it to your device
  2. Make sure:
    • you're in a well lit place
    • all of your fit note is in the image
  3. Save the photo or scan of your fit note somewhere you can easily find on your device
Get more help to get your photo right

If you are having difficulty uploading your fit note, please follow the below instructions

  1. Step 1 Hold the device directly above the fit note

    • it is best to hold the device about 20cm / 1 foot above the fit note
    • avoid taking the photo at an angle
    • hold the device directly above the fit note

  2. Step 2 Find a well lit place and avoid shadows

    2. Make sure:
      • your fit note is well lit
      • the device screen is bright enough to see the image of your fit note clearly
      • nothing is between your fit note and the light to avoid any shadows

  3. Step 3 Make sure your fit note is sideways or 'landscape'

    Ensure that your image includes your whole fit note by holding your device sideways or 'landscape'.

  4. Step 4 Make sure the whole fit note is in the photo

    Ensure that your image includes your whole fit note. It should fill the photo and all 4 corners should be inside it.

  5. Step 5 Make sure the image is clear and not blurred

    A clear image will help us to better access your fit note.

    If you are not sure how to get a clear, sharp image, please check your device manual.

Your photo or scan will be automatically checked to make sure it's good enough.

My doctor sent me an SMS link with my fit note

  1. Open your SMS sent to you by your doctor containing your fit note
  2. Click on the link within your SMS from your doctor
  3. Download your fit note onto your device
    • for more information of how to download your fit note, please look at your device manual
    • please avoid taking a screenshot
  4. Upload your fit note
    • your fit note may be saved in your device's Downloads, Recents or Saved As folder

My doctor sent an email with my fit note attached

  1. Open your email sent to you by your doctor containing your fit note
  2. Download the fit note which was attached from your doctor's email
  3. Upload your fit note
    • your fit note may be saved in your device's Downloads, Recents or Saved As folder
  • The selected file must be a JPG, BMP, PNG, TIF or PDF.
  • The selected file must be smaller than 10MB.
  • Do not upload a screenshot of your fit note as it will not work.
