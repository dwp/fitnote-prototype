Only a fit note can be uploaded and processed by this service

If your document does not look like a fit note it could be an alternative form of medical evidence such as:

  • a medical letter which will have your medical information and details of your hospital or practice
  • an isolation note which will state your start and end date of your isolation period
  • a MED10, which will have dates of your treatment in hospital

Post medical evidence

If you have alternative medical evidence, post to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp. Ensure you keep a copy of any medical evidence for your own record.
