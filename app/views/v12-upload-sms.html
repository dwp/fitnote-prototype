Upload your fit note

Follow these instructions to upload your fit note:

  1. Open your text message sent to you by your doctor or GP practice containing your fit note.
  2. Click on the link within your text from your doctor or GP practice.
  3. Download your fit note onto your device. This may automatically be added to your 'Downloads', 'Recents' or 'Saved As' folder.
  4. Upload your fit note.

Choose a file to upload

Your photo will go through a basic technical check.

In order to upload your fit note successfully:

  • the file must be a JPG, BMP. PNG, TIF or PDF
  • the file must be smaller than 10MB
  • all 4 corners of the fit note must be within the file
I am having difficulty in uploading my fit note

If you have difficulty in uploading your fit note, post your fit note to: Freepost, DWP, ESA 26. You do not need a postage stamp. Ensure you keep a copy of your fit note for your own record.
