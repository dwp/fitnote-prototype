Save and upload your fit note

  1. Open your email sent to you by your GP practice containing your fit note
  2. Download the fit note which was attached from your GP practice's email
    • you may need to click on Download
  3. Click on Choose File below to upload your fit note
    • your fit note may be saved in your Downloads folder

Your photo or scan will be automatically checked to make sure it's good enough.

  • Please ensure that your file upload format is either PDF, JPEG or PNG.
  • The recommended file size is between 500KB and 10MB.
  • Please avoid uploading a screenshot of your fit note.
