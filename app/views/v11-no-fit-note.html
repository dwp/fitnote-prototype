Only a fit note can be uploaded and processed by this service

If your document does not look like a fit note it could be an alternative form of medical evidence, such as:

  • a medical letter which will have details of your hospital or practice
  • an isolation note stating dates of your isolation period
  • a MED10 including dates of your treatment

Post medical evidence

If you have alternative medical evidence, post to:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy of any medical evidence for your own record.
