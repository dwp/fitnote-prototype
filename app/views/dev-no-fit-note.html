We can only accept a fit note (or a Statement of fitness for work)

If your document is not a fit note, it could be an alternative form of medical evidence such as a medical letter, an isolation note or a MED 10.

These are not fit notes and cannot be uploaded onto this service.

If you require a fit note, contact your doctor or GP practice.

Where to send your medical evidence

If you have alternative medical evidence, post it directly to ESA using the address:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp. Make sure you keep a copy of any medical evidence for your own record.
