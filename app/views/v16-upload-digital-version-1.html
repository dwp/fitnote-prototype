Before you upload your digital fit note

  1. Make sure your fit note is landscape (horizontal) not portrait (vertical).
  2. The whole fit note must be shown. It needs to be on 1 page.
  3. Check that it is in focus and all the text can be read.
  4. It may be necessary to take a photo or crop it to create a landscape version.
