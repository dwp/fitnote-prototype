Only a Statement of Fitness for work can be uploaded

Other medical evidence can be only be sent to us by post.

This includes:

  • Medical letter
  • Med10 (hospital certificate)
  • MatB1 (if you are pregnant)
  • CF73 (a form from a psychiatric hospital)

If you want a Statement of fitness for work, you will need to get one from your GP.

Where to send your medical evidence:

  • Freepost DWP
  • ESA 26

You do not need a postage stamp.

Make sure you keep a copy of any medical evidence for your own records.
