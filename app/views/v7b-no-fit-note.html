Only a fit note can be uploaded and processed by this service

If you do not have a fit note, please arrange an appointment with your doctor. The following examples are a medical letter, isolation note and MED 10, they are not fit notes and cannot be uploaded onto this service.

If you have alternative medical evidence, you can send it to Freepost DWP ESA 16. Please ensure that you have a copy or duplicate of your medical evidence before sending it.

How to post medical evidence
Medical evidence can be posted directly to ESA using the address Freepost DWP ESA 26. You do not need a postage stamp.
