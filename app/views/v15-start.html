You will need to send your fit note as part of your application for Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or new style ESA.

You will need:

  • a phone or tablet with a camera, or a computer with a saved photo of your fit note
  • your fit note signed by your doctor
  • your National Insurance number

You do not need to send a fit note if you cannot work while you are self-isolating because of coronavirus (COVID-19).

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
